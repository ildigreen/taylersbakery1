Our Story

Welcome to Taylor's Bakery, a charming establishment nestled in the heart of Odd Down, Bath, where each delectable creation is a testament to Taylor's lifelong love of baking. Taylor, the proud owner and driving force behind the bakery, embarked on this culinary journey inspired by cherished memories of baking with her family during the formative years. The flour-dusted kitchen countertops of her childhood paved the way for a passion that would define her future, and today Taylor's dream of owning a bakery has come true.

Taylor's journey from the family kitchen to the thriving heart of Odd Down is marked by both tradition and modern flair. A hometown hero, Taylor not only started this bakery but also made waves on the national stage, securing a commendable third place in the Great British Bake Off. This achievement not only brought recognition to Taylor but also elevated the bakery's commitment to delivering quality baked goods. Dedication, creativity, and craftsmanship fuel every pastry, cake, and loaf that emerges from Taylor's oven.

Join us in celebrating the story behind the sweetness, where every bite is not just a taste of exceptional flavour but a piece of Taylor's remarkable journey in the world of baking. Welcome to Taylor's Bakery, where every pastry tells a story, and each delightful treat is a chapter in our flavourful biography.
